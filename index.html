Brachot 3a: Continuing the Discussion on Evening Shema

מאימתי קורין את שמע בערבין

Translation: From what time may one recite the Shema in the evening?

The Main Question

The Mishnah asks: From what time can we recite the evening Shema?

משעה שהכהנים נכנסים לאכול בתרומתן

Translation: From the time when the priests enter to eat their terumah.

First Opinion

From the time when the priests enter to eat their terumah (special food for priests).

עד סוף האשמורה הראשונה

Translation: Until the end of the first watch.

Rabban Gamliel's Opinion

Until midnight.

עד שיעלה עמוד השחר

Translation: Until the break of dawn.

The Sages' Opinion

Until dawn.

חצות לילה אקום להודות לך

Translation: At midnight I arise to give thanks unto Thee. (Psalms 119:62)

Proof from King David

The Gemara brings a proof from Psalms where David says he wakes up at midnight to praise God.

שלש משמרות הוי הלילה

Translation: The night consists of three watches.

Discussion: Night Watches

The rabbis discuss how the night is divided into watches and what happens during each watch.

שואג כארי ואומר אוי לבנים

Translation: [God] roars like a lion and says: "Woe to the children."
